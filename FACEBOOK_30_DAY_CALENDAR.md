## Image / video brief notes

For every post, the visual should have:
- ⚡ Yellow accent (your brand color #EAB308)
- Logo top-left or watermark bottom-right
- Phone number visible whenever possible
- Big readable text (most users scroll fast)
- High contrast for mobile screens

For Reels:
- Vertical 9:16
- Captions burned in (75% watch muted)
- First 1.5s = the hook
- Always end with "WhatsApp en bio" or phone number
