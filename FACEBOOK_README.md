## 🔑 The 5 most important things in this entire kit

If you only do five things, do these:

### 1. Optimize the page name + bio (today, 5 minutes)
"BaezMultiservicios" → "MultiServicios El Seibo - Electricista Neno Báez"
Single biggest lever for local SEO. See `FACEBOOK_SETUP_MANUAL.md` Step 1.

### 2. Set up the auto-reply (today, 10 minutes)
Greeting + Away message + 10 FAQ buttons.
This converts inbound DMs while you sleep. See `FACEBOOK_AUTOMATION_CONFIG.md` Parts 1–3.

### 3. Post 5 strong before/afters in the first 14 days (3 hours total)
Real jobs > polished marketing. Phone photos are fine.
Use template captions from `FACEBOOK_30_DAY_CALENDAR.md` Pillar 2.

### 4. Reply to EVERY review within 24 hours (ongoing)
Even 5-stars. Especially negative ones. The response is read by 10x more people than the review itself.
Templates in `FACEBOOK_GROWTH_STRATEGY.md` section 7.

### 5. Run Click-to-WhatsApp ads at $5/day starting Week 4 (ongoing)
The single highest-ROI ad type for local services in 2026.
Full setup in `FACEBOOK_ADS_PLAYBOOK.md` Campaign 1.

## 💡 Red flags — stop and ask if any of these happen

- Account suddenly restricted or warned
- Post reach drops to 0 (might be shadowbanned)
- Negative review goes viral
- A competitor copies your page
- Meta requires identity verification

In any of these cases — pause activity, document everything, follow the crisis playbook in `FACEBOOK_GROWTH_STRATEGY.md` section 11.

## 📞 The bottom line

Facebook growth for a local electrician is **not magic**. It's:
- Show up consistently (post 4-7x/week, stories every day)
- Reply fast (DMs within 15 min, comments within 1 hour)
- Show real work (before/afters > stock photos)
- Get reviews (every job → review request)
- Spend a little ($5–10/day) on the right ad type once foundation is solid

Do this for 90 days and you'll have the dominant electrician page in El Seibo. Do it for 12 months and you'll have more leads than you can handle.
