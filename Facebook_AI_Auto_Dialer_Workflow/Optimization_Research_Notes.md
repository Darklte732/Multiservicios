# Facebook Final Expense AI Auto Dialer - Optimized Workflow

## 📊 **Research-Based Optimizations**

### **Optimal Calling Times for Final Expense Leads (Eastern Time):**
Based on industry research, the best calling times for seniors/final expense leads are:
- **Morning**: 9:00 AM - 11:30 AM (highest answer rates)
- **Early Afternoon**: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM (second best)
- **Early Evening**: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM (good for working seniors)
- **Avoid**: Before 9 AM, 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM (lunch), after 8 PM, Sundays

### **Enhanced Retry Logic:**
Research shows final expense leads require persistence:
- **Attempt 1**: Immediate (during optimal hours)
- **Attempt 2**: 2-4 hours later (different time window)
- **Attempt 3**: Next day, optimal time (24-48 hours)
- **Attempt 4**: 3-5 days later (many seniors need time to consider)

### **High-Converting Follow-up Content:**
Based on successful final expense messaging patterns:

**SMS Tone**: Respectful, helpful, not pushy. Focus on "peace of mind" and "family protection"
**Email Tone**: Educational, compassionate, emphasizing dignity and family financial security
**Key Phrases**: "protect your family", "peace of mind", "dignity", "final wishes", "no burden"
